Giorgia Insalata

Giorgia is a self taught London born artist with Italian and Zimbabwean heritage.

Her art focuses on identity, relationships and black femininity explored through portraiture. The main theme of her work is the re-contextualisation of classical and allegorical figures that portray the modern day black female experience, using colour and symbolism.

The Artist is interested in exploring the common experience of black women in Europe and infusing them with Greek mythology and biblical references.

These works seek to represent through the lenses of classical paintings. Her main inspiration is taken from her life in boarding schools around England and growing up as a young Italian woman of colour in both London and Italy.
